1. Who We Are

Navigator Builds is operated by Nenad Vasic, based in Montenegro. This policy covers navigatorbuilds.com and all digital products sold through it.

2. What We Collect

When you visit the website: Nothing. This site has no analytics, no cookies, no tracking scripts. Zero telemetry.

When you purchase a product: Paddle (our payment processor and Merchant of Record) collects your name, email address, and payment information. We receive your name and email for order fulfillment. We do not see or store your payment details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver your purchased products (download links)
  • Send product update notifications (only for products you purchased)
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone except Paddle for payment processing.

4. Our Products and Your Data

Elara Core and all configuration products run entirely on your local machine. No data is sent to us or any third party. Your memories, conversations, mood data, and all cognitive artifacts stay on your device. Always.

5. Paddle (Payment Processor)

Payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Ltd, who acts as the Merchant of Record. Paddle has their own privacy policy that applies to payment transactions. Visit paddle.com/privacy for details.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as your purchase is active (for update notifications). You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of update notifications

Email nenadvasic@protonmail.com for any privacy requests.
